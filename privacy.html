Inkwery Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 23, 2025

At Inkwery, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and safeguard your data when you use our platform at inkwery.netlify.app. By using Inkwery, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information to provide and improve our services:

  • Personal Information: When you sign up or log in, we collect your name, email address, and password. You may also provide additional details, such as a profile picture or bio, if available on the platform.
  • Content You Share: Any content you post, such as articles, images, or other contributions, along with associated metadata (e.g., post date, author ID).
  • Usage Data: We collect information about how you interact with our platform, including IP address, browser type, device information, pages visited, and timestamps.
  • Cookies and Tracking: We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience, analyze usage, and deliver personalized content. You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data to:

  • Provide and maintain the Inkwery platform, including user accounts and content sharing features.
  • Personalize your experience, such as displaying your posts or recommending content.
  • Communicate with you, including sending account-related notifications or responding to inquiries.
  • Analyze usage to improve our services, troubleshoot issues, and enhance security.
  • Comply with legal obligations, such as responding to lawful requests or protecting our rights.

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your data in the following cases:

  • Public Content: Content you post (e.g., articles, images) is visible to other users and may be publicly accessible, depending on platform settings.
  • Service Providers: We work with third-party providers (e.g., Firebase for authentication, Netlify for hosting) who process data on our behalf under strict confidentiality agreements.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose data if required by law, such as in response to a court order or government request.
  • Business Transfers: If Inkwery is involved in a merger or acquisition, your data may be transferred to the new entity, with continued privacy protections.

4. Your Choices and Rights

You have control over your data:

  • Account Management: You can update or delete your account information through your profile settings.
  • Content Removal: You can delete your posts at any time, subject to our Terms of Service.
  • Cookies: You can disable cookies via your browser, though this may affect platform functionality.
  • Data Access: You may request access to or a copy of your personal data by contacting us at iamaryavam@gmail.com.
  • Opt-Out: You can opt out of non-essential communications, such as promotional emails, via links in our emails.

Users in certain regions (e.g., EU under GDPR) may have additional rights, such as data portability or erasure. Contact us to exercise these rights.

5. Data Security

We use industry-standard measures to protect your data, including encryption for data in transit and at rest. However, no system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security. Please use strong passwords and keep your account credentials confidential.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only as long as necessary for the purposes outlined in this policy or as required by law. Account data is deleted upon account deletion, though public content may remain if shared by others. Usage data is retained for analytics purposes but anonymized where possible.

7. Third-Party Links

Inkwery may contain links to third-party websites (e.g., social media platforms). We are not responsible for their privacy practices. Please review their policies before sharing data.

8. Children’s Privacy

Inkwery is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe we have such data, please contact us to have it removed.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify users of significant changes via email or a notice on the platform. The “Last Updated” date indicates the latest revision.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

  • Website: inkwery.netlify.app
