Who We Are

Founded in 2025 by Aryan Singh, Inkwery is India’s premier platform for creators and enthusiasts to connect, collaborate, and showcase their creativity. Based in India, we are passionate about building a vibrant community where ideas come to life. Whether you’re an artist, writer, designer, or innovator, Inkwery provides a space to share your work, engage with others, and grow your audience.

Our platform is designed to be intuitive and accessible, with features that make it easy to create, share, and support creative projects. We aim to empower creators by offering tools to express their vision and, where applicable, monetize their work through secure and seamless payment options.

Our Mission

Our mission is to foster creativity and build a supportive ecosystem for Indian creators. We believe in the power of creative expression to inspire, connect, and transform communities. Inkwery is here to provide the tools, resources, and opportunities for creators to thrive.

Our Values

  • Creativity: We celebrate originality and encourage creators to push boundaries.
  • Community: We foster a collaborative and inclusive environment for all users.
  • Trust: We prioritize user privacy, security, and transparency in all interactions.
  • Accessibility: Our platform is designed to be user-friendly and inclusive for creators of all backgrounds.

How Inkwery Works

Inkwery connects creators and enthusiasts through a seamless platform where you can:

  • Create and share content, such as artwork, writing, or projects, with a vibrant community.
  • Engage with other creators through comments, likes, or collaborations.
  • Support creators through secure payment features, such as subscriptions or one-time contributions (where available).
  • Access tools to manage your profile, track engagement, and grow your creative presence.

Our payment features, powered by trusted third-party providers, ensure a secure and frictionless experience, allowing creators to focus on their craft while reaching their audience effectively.

Why Choose Inkwery?

Inkwery stands out because:

  • We are built for India’s creative community, with a deep understanding of local talent and culture.
  • Our platform is simple, fast, and mobile-friendly, ensuring accessibility for all users.
  • We prioritize security, using encryption and trusted payment gateways to protect your data and transactions.
  • We are committed to empowering creators with tools to succeed and connect globally.
